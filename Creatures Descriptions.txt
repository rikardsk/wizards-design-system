This is an excellent progression. Goblins are foundational to high fantasy, and a standard Dragon is the ultimate MTG Level 4 threat for mountains.

To preserve your tactile **Classic High Fantasy Romanticism** aesthetic for the web-based game, I have designed these tokens as **2.5D "Digital Standees."**

This means they are not flat digital conceptual drawings. They are rendered with rich, heavy oil brushwork and are framed by a thick, sculpted, gilded-gold **hexagonal impasto edge**, which precisely matches the borders of your terrain tiles. They cast a gentle dropshadow onto the hex map, giving them physical weight.

Here are 4 levels of creatures per terrain type, designed to fit this premium digital-physical aesthetic.

---

### Terrain Type I: Mountain (The Granite Gray / Ochre Hex)

*mtg Philosophy: Chaos, direct damage, and formidable raw power.*

1. **Level 1: Skirk Prospector Goblin.** (As requested)
**Visual Description:** A digital standee featuring a squat, grinning goblin with cracked, emerald-green skin. It is clad in mismatched leather scrap-armor. The illustration uses thick impasto strokes, catching dramatic, warm orange sunlight filtering through rocks. It is framed by the heavy gilded-gold hexagonal border.
2. **Level 2: Ogre Sunderer.**
**Visual Description:** A large, brutish ogre with granite-gray skin that matches the terrain hex. It is shown wielding a colossal tree-trunk club. The oil brushwork emphasizes the creature's thick muscles and scarred hide, contrasting warm reddish skin with cool blue-gray rock.
3. **Level 3: Magma Rifter Elemental.**
**Visual Description:** An elemental creature made of cooling, dark basalt rock. Fissures across its entire body glow with intense, internal molten gold and orange light. The artwork features strong chiaroscuro, casting deep, jagged shadows.
4. **Level 4: Hellkite Ancient (Dragon).** (As requested)
**Visual Description:** A colossal dragon with crimson and soot-black scales. It dominates the token, depicted in a raised Isometric profile, looking down. Visible heavy brushwork captures the scale texture and the intense, burning fire glowing within its throat, casting a warm golden aura around its head. The token feels heavy.

### Terrain Type II: Plain (The Golden Wheat / Farmland Hex)

*mtg Philosophy: Order, community, and defensive strength.*

1. **Level 1: Field Sentry Human.**
**Visual Description:** A human soldier clad in polished steel chainmail. The oil painting style captures the soft, dapple golden light of dusk filtering through clouds, reflecting off their helmet. They hold a pike, looking watchful.
2. **Level 2: Leonin Sun-Stalker (Cat Warrior).**
**Visual Description:** A majestic, bipedal lion warrior with a golden-brown mane. They wear refined, bronze-colored armor. Chiaroscuro lighting isolates them, illuminated by a harsh, warm directional light from one side, casting a long shadow.
3. **Level 3: Griffin Hatchery Guardian.**
**Visual Description:** A fierce griffin (half-eagle, half-lion). The illustration features rich, sweeping paint texture on the white and gold wing feathers, capturing the bird’s musculature as it perches, guarding an unseen nest.
4. **Level 4: Archon of Grace.**
**Visual Description:** An archon (an armored angelic being) riding a winged, crystalline ram. The token is bathed in cool, ethereal pale blue and soft purple internal light. The artwork is sleek and elegant, contrasted against the massive, majestic gilded hexagonal border.

### Terrain Type III: Forest (The Emerald / Deep Moss Hex)

*mtg Philosophy: Growth, instinct, and enormous size.*

1. **Level 1: Leaf-Crowned Elf Scout.**
**Visual Description:** A slender elven figure with forest-green skin, adorned with woven leaf-armor that seems alive. They are illuminated by cool, dapple green light filtering through an invisible canopy, highlighted by visible impasto strokes.
2. **Level 2: Rootwalla Lizard.**
**Visual Description:** A robust lizard that can instantly inflate its body into a defensive spiky ball. The oil brushwork captures the thick, scaled texture and the sudden change in volume, emphasizing rich umber and emerald green tones.
3. **Level 3: Heartwood Dryad.**
**Visual Description:** A dryad whose body is seamlessly woven from twisted, gnarled tree roots and bark (matching Level 3 Forest hexes). Subtle, warm orange and pale blue light emanates from hidden bioluminescent moss patches deep within their form.
4. **Level 4: Sylvan Primordial (Giant Treefold).**
**Visual Description:** A colossal, walking treefold with massive, twisted roots forming its legs. The illustration is defined by its scale; its canopy is so dense it blocks all natural light. Glimmers of misty, internal green light define the massive structure.

### Terrain Type IV: Swamp (The Murky Olive / Burnt Umber Hex)

*mtg Philosophy: Death, sacrifice, and toxic decay.*

1. **Level 1: Festering Bog-Rot Human Zombie.**
**Visual Description:** A standard, gray-skinned zombie rising from dark, tea-colored water. Its clothing is ragged. The painting style features flat, cloudy light, casting deep shadows in the zombie's features.
2. **Level 2: Carrion Crow Swarm.**
**Visual Description:** A dense standee representing a swarm of large, oily-black crows. The oil brushwork creates texture on the dark feathers, catching subtle, eerie green reflections. The token casting a shifting dropshadow.
3. **Level 3: Fen Haunt Spirit.**
**Visual Description:** A wraith-like spirit whose form is made of murky oil-slick black water and dark peat (matching Level 2 and 3 Swamp tiles). Faint, eerie green wisps of gas gently drift from the token frame, which pulses with pale bioluminescence.
4. **Level 4: Mire Monarch (Colossal Toad).**
**Visual Description:** A massive, grotesque toad with warty, murky olive skin. It is partially submerged in black water. Eerie, glowing gaseous green wisps and bubbles rise from the token’s surface, casting a strange light on the token frame.
